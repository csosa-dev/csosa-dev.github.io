GS-COM KANO Gaming PC

Overview

Introducing the GS-COM KANO GAMING PC – an entry-level powerhouse designed for performance at an affordable price. Featuring the AMD RYZEN 5 5500 6-Core 4.2GHz processor and MSI GeForce RTX™ 3050 VENTUS 2X XS 8G OC graphics card, this build delivers excellent gaming performance. The Kingston Fury Beast RGB 8GB 3200MHz DDR4 memory provides lightning-fast speeds, while the MSI B450M-A PRO MAX II motherboard ensures robust and reliable connectivity. With the Intel 670p Series 512GB PCIe NVMe SSD, you'll enjoy rapid load times and ample storage space. The MSI MAG A550BN 550W power supply allows you to overclock your CPU without blowing it up and the Belize power grid!
